Engineering

Engineering is the core of our institution, without it, we wouldn't be where we are today. Over the past few years, our engineering program has grown and innovated to a national level allowing students to discover, and pave their own path in the field. Please join us in our journey where we show what this program is made of and the people who make it possible.

Mission Statement

The Science Academy of South Texas offers a curriculum of choice with an emphasis on Science, Mathematics, and Engineering that fosters curiosity, open mindedness and a passion for life-long learning.

Vision Statement

Inspiring students to problem-solve, innovate and transform their communities
The Science Academy is a TEA Exemplary campus and a nationally recognized Blue Ribbon School.
Partnerships with Rice University, Rochester Institute of Technology, University of Texas-Rio Grande Valley, South Texas College, Johns Hopkins University, Cornell University, Stanford University

Engineering at Scitech

Over the past 20 years, the Science Academy has been in partnership with Project Lead the Way by integrating its engineering curriculum. Its program fosters student engagement and learning in an engineering environment, while also having the opportunity to earn college credit. The PLTW student engagement philosophy is integrated with every engineering course offered at our school. Discover our engineering program below. Click the logos to learn more.

EDD

Engineering Design and Development is the baby of the Science Academy. This course has given birth to innovators, millionaires, and a nationwide course that's being adopted by all high schools.

GEMS

GEMS (Girls Excelling in Math and Science) has been working since 1994 to increase interest in STEM for girls in elementary and middle school and to expose girls to the fun and wonder of these fields.
