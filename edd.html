Engineering Design and Development

Since the school was established, our original teachers got together and wanted to establish a course that would distinguish students from others, as a result the "Research and Design program was established". This program required students to solve a common problem in our world using their previous knowledge acquired in their past 3 years of engineering coursework. The results of this program has been astonishing. Multiple patents have been filed on some of the ideas that students created, and better yet in 1998 PLTW asked the Science Academy to incorporate this course into their national curriculum. There is no doubt that EDD has made a significant impact and will continue to do so.
